      Frequently Asked Questions

      Have a question about one of our Tecnológico de Monterrey Boot Camps? We’re here to answer.

      Learn more about our boot camp offerings below.

      If you prefer, click here to chat live with our admissions team »
      What is a boot camp?
      • Boot camps are intensive, fast-paced learning programs that teach digital skills like web development to beginners. Students learn a wide variety of skills in a short amount of time. Boot camps have a skills-based curriculum, which gives students real-world experience building projects and applications. This allows students to finish the program ready to enter the tech field.
      What are the dates of Tecnológico de Monterrey Coding Boot Camp?
      • Classes begin multiple times per year. Please visit our schedule page for details.
      • Classes are held Mondays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays from 6:30 – 9:30 p.m.
      Can I keep working while studying in the program?
      • Yes. Our program has a part-time schedule designed for working adults, students, and busy professionals.
      What is the tuition cost of the program?
      • The tuition fee is MXN $100,995 plus 16 % IVA. Students can also qualify for an interest-free payment plan, allowing you to pay the tuition in fixed installments.
      How long is the course?
      • Students can complete the program in six months.
      Do you provide career planning support?
      • Yes. We offer all of our students career support and coaching, including portfolio reviews, resume assistance, and technical interview training. In addition, we offer students experiential learning opportunities to strengthen their portfolio.
      Do I need to have previous experience in programming before enrolling?
      • No. While having some background can help, our pre-course tutorials will get you up to speed and ready to start learning on Day 1.
      What criteria do you look for in potential students applying to the program?
      • We believe the main criterion for success in our program is your passion and commitment to succeed. Learning web development is difficult and requires hours of hard work, and we look for applicants who are determined and willing to give the program their all in order to achieve life-changing results.
      Do I need to be a Tecnológico de Monterrey student to apply?
      • No, applicants do not need to be Tecnológico de Monterrey students.
      Is there an application process? How do I get started?
      • Our program has an application process and is not open enrollment. Once a prospective student fills out the form or calls our admissions department, they will go through an initial phone interview with an advisor. Applicants who are recommended to move forward will then take a critical thinking and problem-solving assessment.
      What sets your boot camp apart from others in Mexico City or surrounding areas?
      • Our program provides options to best suit your lifestyle, with in-person and online formats in a part-time schedule.
      • We offer a part-time class schedule that allows working professionals to study web development without leaving work.
      • Students benefit from a robust set of professional development services to prepare them for success as they work to advance or change careers.
      • Our curriculum is built based on the most in-demand technologies specific to the regional market.
      How many hours a week is the program?
      • You can expect to spend approximately 9 hours per week in class. To be successful, our students spend an average of an additional 20 hours per week in class preparation, homework and career services assignments.
      Do I need to bring my own computer?
      • Yes, a reliable laptop is required for this course. Laptops can be Mac or PC, with a minimum 8GB of RAM and a dual core processor (64 bit).
      • If you’re unsure if your laptop meets the requirements, please check with our admissions advisor first and they will make sure you’re good to go.
      Will I need to purchase books?
      • No, all that is needed for class is your laptop and the eagerness to learn.
      What financial support is available?
      • Students can explore Banregio: Banregio offers advantageous loans for students taking our course.
      • Students can arrange to set up a payment plan.* To find out more, call 55-8525-0340 to speak to an Admissions Advisor.
      • Tecnológico de Monterrey Alumni* receive a tuition discount of MXN $5,000.
      • Current students* of Tecnológico de Monterrey receive a tuition discount of MXN $5,000.
      • Individuals who enroll by the early registration deadline* and pay in full are eligible for a discount of MXN $5,000.

      *Offers cannot be combined.

      How do I prove my level of proficiency in English?
      • The program is delivered in English and students must demonstrate the necessary level of English to be successful in the boot camp. You will be able to demonstrate this through an interview in English conducted by your admissions advisor. For more information, please contact your admissions advisor.
      I’m an employer, how can I hire one of your graduates or become an employer partner?
      • We’re so glad you asked. If you’re interested in partnering with our bootcamp or accessing our student portal, please fill out this form and a member of our career services team will get in touch with you.
      What are the dates of Tecnológico de Monterrey Data Analytics Boot Camp?
      • Classes begin quarterly. Please visit our schedule page for details.
      Can I keep working while studying in the program?
      • Yes. The program was designed for working professionals who are seeking to advance their current careers or change paths.
      How long does the program take to complete?
      • The program is part-time. Students can complete it in six months (24 weeks).
      What is the tuition cost of the program?
      • The tuition fee is MXN $100,995 plus 16 % IVA. Students can also qualify for an interest-free payment plan, allowing you to pay the tuition in fixed installments.
      Do you provide career planning support?
      • Yes. We offer all of our students career support and coaching as well as resume and portfolio support, interview preparation and experiential learning opportunities.
      Do I need to have previous experience in programming or data analytics before applying?
      • No programming experience is required, however it is recommended that applicants hold a Bachelor’s Degree or have at least two years of experience in business, management, finance, statistics, or a related field.
      What criteria do you look for in potential students applying to the program?
      • We believe the main criterion for success in our program is your passion and commitment to succeed. Learning data analytics is difficult and requires hours of hard work, and we look for applicants who are determined and willing to give the program their all in order to achieve life-changing results.
      Do I need to be a Tecnológico de Monterrey student to apply?
      • No, applicants do not need to be Tecnológico de Monterrey students.
      Is there an application process? How do I get started?
      • Our program has an application process and is not open enrollment. Once a prospective student fills out the form or calls our admissions department, they will go through an initial phone interview with an advisor. Applicants who are recommended to move forward will then take a critical thinking and problem-solving assessment.
      What sets your boot camp apart from others I can join in Mexico City or elsewhere?
      • We offer a part-time program that allows working professionals to study data analytics and visualization without leaving their current job.
      • Students benefit from a robust set of professional development services to prepare them for success as they work to advance or change careers.
      • Our curriculum is built based on the most in-demand technologies specific to the market.
      How many hours a week is the program?
      • Our part-time program requires 9 hours of in-class time per week. In addition, you must be willing to dedicate a minimum of 20+ hours to self-paced content per week in order to truly immerse yourself in the materials covered in class.
      Do I need to bring my own computer?
      • Yes, a reliable laptop is required for this course. Laptops can be Mac or PC, with a minimum 8GB of RAM and a dual core processor (64 bit). Microsoft Excel 2016 or later is also required.
      • If you’re unsure if your laptop meets the requirements, please check with our admissions advisor first and they will make sure you’re good to go.
      Will I need to purchase books?
      • No, all that is needed for class is your laptop and the eagerness to learn.
      What financial support is available?
      • Students can explore Banregio: Banregio offers advantageous loans for students taking our course.
      • Students can arrange to set up a payment plan. To find out more, call 55-8525-0340 to speak to an admissions advisor.

      • Current students of Tecnológico de Monterrey receive a tuition discount of MXN $5,000.
      • Individuals who enroll and pay in full by the early registration deadline are eligible for a MXN $10,000 early registration discount.

      • We’re so glad you asked. If you’re interested in partnering with our boot camp or accessing our student portal, please fill out this form and a member of our career services team will get in touch with you.

You will be able to demonstrate this through an interview in English conducted by your admissions advisor. For more information, please contact your admissions advisor.
